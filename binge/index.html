Binge

Flutter - Android & iOS

Mobile app to keep track of shows you watch and how far you are.

Binge is a show tracking app enables you to keep track of all of your shows regardless of service and platform, and when the time comes to watch you know which show to catch up on and from where; no more time spent on figuring out where you left off last time.

Homepage where users can find shows with new unseen episodes.

Showpage where users can explore the show and mark off episodes.

Episodepage where users can view episodes in detail and mark them off.

Users can quickly search the database for their show or to discover new shows by browsing the popular shows or the recommended section of each show.

Discoverpage where users can discover and explore shows.

Searchpage where users can search for shows and save them.

The recommended section where users might find other shows they might like.

On the statistics page users will find information like number of episodes viewed, hours spent watching and their top 3 most watch show with posibility to expand to show all shows sorted by watchtime.

Statisticspage with a few numbers reflecting users shows.

Rest of Statistics Page with Top 3 Shows and Total Time Watched.

The user's shows sorted by Watch Time.

Some of the users were not impressed with the very purple design of the app, so I created a dark mode that is enabled by default and can be turned off by deactivting force dark mode option in settings and setting the phone/system theme to light theme. (Turing off Force Dark Mode, uses System Theme)

Homepage where users can find shows with new unseen episodes. - Dark Mode

Discoverpage where users can discover and explore shows. - Dark Mode

Showpage where users can explore the show and mark off episodes. - Dark Mode
