Mist

Native Android - Kotlin

Mobile app to check the air quality and weather in Norwegian cities.

Mist shows the air quality in Norwegian cities in a light and user-friendly way with data from the Meteorological Institute's API, it also shows other practical information such as weather, humidity and wind speed.

The loading screen users see while the app is fetching data in the bacjground..

First time user launches the app they are greeted with a Onboarding

The Onboarding can be "Redone" by unchecking the box for don't show Onboarding

Users can quickly search for theirs city or browse through the list of cities.

Once the app is finish fetching users will be presented a list with all cities and key information

They can search for a city in the list and add favorites witch will be pinned.

When users launches the app next time their favorites will be pinned on the top.

The City page display air quality, weather, humidity and wind speed with hour by hour forcast to the users in a nice and user-friendly way

On the city page users can view all the information and look at future forcast as well

The Modal bottom sheet reveals the explanation for all the values

Users can tap the Map box to expand and explore the map that shows status for each base
